About


I'm a seasoned system engineer and researcher, specializing in a diverse array of fields including computer security, hardware design, embedded systems, robotics, and data analysis. My research interests span embedded systems, industrial networks, communication protocols, RF, artificial intelligence, computer security, blockchain technology, and bioinformatics. My expertise encompasses malware analysis, pentesting, data communication, radiofrequency, control theory, sensor data acquisition, digital filters, computer vision, path planning, ROS for drones, blockchain applications and simulations in bioinformatics.

With years of experience in backend, infrastructure, and security engineering across sectors such as power, payments, gaming, and healthcare, I've leveraged my skills in security, fault tolerance, real-time systems, automation, and architecture to enhance organizational resilience and efficiency. I'm a licensed radio amateur (PU7OLV), passionate about radio communication. Proficient in C, C++, assembly, Python, Erlang, Lisp, Ruby and Go, I excel in software and hardware reverse engineering, and developing industrial communication protocols.
